01. About Me

I'm a PhD researcher at the Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology (Skoltech) in Moscow, working on Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring (NILM) — the art of disaggregating a building's total energy signal into individual appliance-level consumption.

My journey spans from programming Siemens PLCs on factory floors, to designing solar PV systems across Iran, to now pushing the boundaries of energy AI and smart grid research. I'm also the Project Manager at MoniSensa, where we're building the future of energy monitoring.

I believe in the intersection of signal processing, machine learning, and energy systems — where real impact happens for a sustainable future.
